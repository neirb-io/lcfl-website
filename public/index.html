Family Law Services in Lake City, Florida

Boudreau Law Firm, previously known as Sandow Law Firm, has been representing clients in family law matters throughout Florida for over 40 years. We are dedicated to guiding individuals through complex and often sensitive domestic issues with clarity and care. Our focus is on building lasting client relationships while delivering strong, results-driven representation. Matters such as divorce, paternity, and child custody disputes frequently involve both emotional and financial stress, and we are committed to offering individualized support and practical solutions to help families move forward.

In both contested and uncontested divorce proceedings, our attorneys are experienced in negotiating parenting plans, alimony, child support, division of marital property and debts, and other related concerns. Many of our cases involve complicated financial elements, including business valuations, retirement assets, and pension division. Clients with multiple marriages may face especially challenging financial considerations, and we are equipped to address those complexities. When disputes cannot be resolved through mediation or settlement, we are fully prepared to advocate for our clients in court.

At Boudreau Law Firm, PLLC, we also assist clients in protecting and asserting their parental and custody rights. Our team handles post-judgment modifications stemming from significant life changes, such as relocation or adjustments to alimony and child support. When a former spouse fails to comply with the terms of a final judgment, we represent clients in enforcement and contempt proceedings. In addition, we provide representation in cases involving allegations of domestic violence.
