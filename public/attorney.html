Attorney Gayle Boudreau

Attorney Gayle Boudreau founded Boudreau Law Firm, PLLC in 2021. Before launching her firm, she joined Sandow Law Firm as an associate in February 2020, where she worked closely with Attorney Robert A. Sandow on a wide range of family law matters. Today, Boudreau Law Firm represents clients in divorce, paternity, and other family law cases throughout Florida’s Third Judicial Circuit, which includes seven counties.

Ms. Boudreau earned her Juris Doctor from Florida A&M University College of Law in 2013. During law school, she interned with a federal judge at the United States District Court for the Middle District of Florida and with the United States Attorney’s Office. After being admitted to the Florida Bar, she concentrated on criminal law and served for over five years as an Assistant State Attorney in both the Ninth and Third Judicial Circuits of Florida.

Before pursuing law, Ms. Boudreau obtained a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research from the University of Massachusetts Amherst, as well as a Master of Business Administration from the University of Miami School of Business. She brings more than 35 years of combined experience across engineering, quality assurance, and the legal profession, along with a strong background in leadership. Her diverse expertise in law, technology, and business management allows her to deliver strategic and personalized services to her clients.

Ms. Boudreau discovered her passion for family law while working alongside Attorney Sandow, who has built a 40-year reputation for excellence in the field. He now serves as Attorney of Counsel to Boudreau Law Firm, continuing to contribute his extensive knowledge and experience for the benefit of clients.

She finds great fulfillment in helping individuals and families navigate life transitions and work toward a more secure future. Outside of her legal practice, Ms. Boudreau enjoys spending time with her children, tending to her small farm, and supporting her community. She is an active member of Altrusa International of Lake City and currently serves as Vice-President of the Third Judicial Circuit Bar Association.
